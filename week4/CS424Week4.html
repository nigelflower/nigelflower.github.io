Inverse Correlation between Weight and Acceleration

There is an inverse correlation between weight and acceleration. This is very logical because as the cars increase in weight, it takes more energy to make it accelerate.

Inverse Correlation between HP and Acceleration

There seems to be an inverse correlation between horse power and time for acceleration. This is because the more powerful a car is the less time it takes for it to accelerate.

Inverse Correlation between Displacement and Mileage

There is an inverse correlation between displacement and mileage, because each time the engine rotates, the more gasoline the car needs
