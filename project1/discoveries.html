Discoveries from the Data

Regarding the cholera outbreak in England, the majority of the deaths took place during a week's period, the number of total deaths trailing off afterwards.

The Cholera outbreak in Naples seems to have hit people over the age of 80 the worst.

The deaths in England during the Cholera outbreak seem to circle directly around a single water pump located in Soho. The data points are evenly spread around where the pump is, and the points with the greatest density crowd around it as well.
