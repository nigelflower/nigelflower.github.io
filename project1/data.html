The data used in this project

Datasets

This project was performed using a total of five different datasets

The first dataset relates to a cholera outbreak that took place in England during 1851.

The second dataset was taken durinng a cholera outbreak in Naples and records the age and sex of the victims

Next we have some census data collected in England during 1851

The last two detail the locations of cholera deaths in households surrounding a set of water pumps in London. These data are plotted over a map showing where in London the deaths occured.S
